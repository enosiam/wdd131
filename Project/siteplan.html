Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide visitors with a gallery of The church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints temples worldwide, including historical details, dedication dates, and building sizes. It helps users learn about both older and newer temples and explore them visually.

Scenarios

  • Which temples were built before the year 1900?
  • What are the newest temples dedicated after 2000?
  • Which temples are the largest by square footage?

Color Schema

Two primary colors will be used across the site:

  • Navy Blue (#1E3A8A): Headings, navigation bar, and accents.
  • Light Gray (#F3F4F6): Page background, card backgrounds.

Typography

The site will use the Poppins font family:

  • Headings: Poppins, weight 600 (bold)
  • Body Text: Poppins, weight 400 (regular)
