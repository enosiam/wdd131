LDS Temples

Temple Album

This name represents a collection of Latter-day Saint temples around the world. The site will serve as a reference and gallery. Optional domain: temple-album.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display information and images about temples built by the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Visitors will be able to browse a collection of temples, learn their history, location, and size. Filters will allow users to view temples by age, size, or other categories.

Scenarios

  • Which temples were built before 1900?
  • What is the largest temple in square footage?
  • When was the Accra Ghana Temple dedicated?
